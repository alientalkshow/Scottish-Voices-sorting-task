Participant Information and Research Consent

Project title: Scottish Voices: An Investigation of Perceived Speaker Similarity. (Ethics approval: EMS10022/10040)

Researchers’ names: Marta Brzoska and Hajar Benharraf (Abertay University)

Supervisor: Prof Vera Kempe (Abertay University)

What is the research about?

This research project aims to explore how individuals group Scottish talkers by the way they speak.

Do I have to take part?

Participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time during the study. Once you click ‘Submit Task’, your anonymised data cannot be withdrawn.

What will I be required to do?

You will first answer demographic questions, then listen to voice recordings and group them based on speech similarity. You can replay audio and group however you like.

How will you handle my data?

Your anonymised data will only be accessible to the researchers and stored securely. Abertay University is the Data Controller (DataProtectionOfficer@abertay.ac.uk).

Retention of research data

Anonymised data may be stored indefinitely. Consent forms and related research data will be retained according to institutional policy.
