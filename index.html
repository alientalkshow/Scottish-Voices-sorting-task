Welcome to the Speaker Sorting Task

Instructions

On the next slide, you will see a large grid with grid lines and 12 black rectangles with the initials of 12 speakers who all are saying the same sentence. Once you click on a rectangle you can hear the speaker. Your task is to listen to all speakers and move the rectangles anywhere on the grid to group them by how similar they sound in how they speak. You can listen as often as you want and build as few or many groupings as you like.
